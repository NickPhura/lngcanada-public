This site was developed by the LNG Canada Implementation Secretariat within the Ministry of Energy, Mines and Petroleum Resources.

The Secretariat was established to ensure LNG Canada and its connecting pipeline - Coastal GasLink - continue to meet British Columbia’s four conditions for liquefied natural gas development.

This website is an aspect of the Secretariat’s commitment to provide details about the provincial government’s role as it relates to LNG Canada, including the initial environmental assessment process, engagement with Indigenous Nations, permitting, regulatory oversight and compliance procedures.
